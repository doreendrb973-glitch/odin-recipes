Shrimp Alfredo Pasta

Description

A wonderful shrimp pasta dish!

I came up with this on my own one night. You can change the amount of ingredients to your taste.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb shrimp
  • 1 lb pasta
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta according to package instructions.
  2. Shrimp in a large skillet over medium-high heat until pink.
  3. Add garlic and cook for 1 minute.
  4. Pour in heavy cream and bring to a simmer.
  5. Stir in parmesan cheese until melted.
  6. Combine the shrimp and Alfredo sauce with the cooked pasta.
