Brownie

Description

A wonderful brownie!

fudgy brownies anytime with this pantry-staple, reliable recipe.

Home cooks rave about the dense, chewy texture of these brownies, calling it better than store-bought.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour and cocoa powder.
  3. In a separate bowl, cream the butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
  4. Beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla extract.
  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until just combined.
  6. Pour the batter into a greased 8x8 inch baking pan.
  7. Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean .
