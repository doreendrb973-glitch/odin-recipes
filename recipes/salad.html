Chicken Caesar Salad

Description

A wonderful chicken caesar salad!

This recipe boasts a bold, rich flavor thanks to anchovies, garlic, lemon, mustard, and Parmesan.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb chicken breast
  • 1 head romaine lettuce
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 6 cloves garlic
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 5 anchovy fillets
  • 1 lemon
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Grill or pan-sear the chicken breast until fully cooked.
  2. Wash and chop the romaine lettuce.
  3. Mix the heavy cream, mayonnaise, anchovies, lemon juice, mustard,
    Worcestershire sauce, olive oil, and parmesan cheese in a bowl.
  4. Combine the chicken and lettuce with the dressing.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Serve immediately and enjoy!
